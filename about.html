Shaping things for web

I am a passionate junior front-end developer with positive attitude. I geek out about things like clean code, new tools and libraries.

The one thing that keeps pushing me into continuous development of my tech skills is the constant change of technology.

The reason I created this website was to better display my work and possibly find new projects.
